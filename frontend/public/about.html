📍 Mekan

AI-Powered Place Discovery Platform

Don't just count the stars — look at real satisfaction scores. Mekan analyzes reviews with AI to give you an honest picture.

✨ Features

🤖

AI Review Analysis

Every review is run through Gemini AI for sentiment analysis and content moderation. Fake or malicious reviews are automatically flagged.

🗺️

Map-Based Discovery

See what's around you on an interactive Leaflet-powered map. Filter and sort to find exactly what you're looking for.

🏆

AI Satisfaction Score

Not just a star average — a composite score built from sentiment analysis, ratings, and positive review ratio.

⭐

Favorite Places

Save your favorite places and stay up to date with any changes.

🔥

Trend Tracking

Track the places getting the most traction in the last 7 days, in real time.

🛡️

Content Moderation

Approve reviews via the admin panel and get AI-powered reply suggestions.

⚙️ How It Works

1

User writes a review

After finding a place on the map, the user enters a rating and comment. The form is rate-limited to prevent abuse.

2

Gemini AI analyzes it

The review is sent to the Gemini 1.5 Flash model for sentiment detection (positive/negative/neutral), satisfaction scoring, and inappropriate content checks.

3

AI makes a decision

Clean reviews are published automatically. Suspicious content is sent to the admin review queue.

4

Score is calculated and published

AI score + star average + positive review ratio produce a composite satisfaction percentage. Rankings and "Place of the Month" are determined by this value.
